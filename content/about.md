Team Phynx is the development identity behind Soulior.

Soulior is created by Team Phynx.

The project is currently focused on learning, prototyping, and building a clear foundation for a future 2D action game. That means the public site is meant to show real progress, real limitations, and a consistent development rhythm.

## Team Phynx

Team Phynx - Indie game developer team (solo team btw)

Team Phynx is the name used for the public-facing development work around Soulior. It exists to give the project a clear identity while development is still early.

## Lazy Cat

Lazy Cat is the one of creator behind Soulior. The work at this stage is practical: defining scope, building prototypes, and understanding what kind of action game Soulior should become.

## Current Focus

- Learning through development
- Building a clear prototype foundation
- Documenting progress through sprint devlogs
- Keeping scope realistic while the game direction is still being tested
