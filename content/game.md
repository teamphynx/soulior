## What is Soulior?

Soulior is a 2D action game project by Team Phynx. The current work is focused on building a clear gameplay foundation, testing ideas early, and learning through steady development.

## Current Stage

Soulior is in the idea and early prototype preparation stage. The project is using Godot 4 and working in 2-week sprints with temporary prototype assets.

## Core Ideas

- Responsive 2D action
- Strong soul theme atmosphere with Japanese ref.
- Small, testable prototype scope
- Iteration through short sprint cycles

## Prototype Disclaimer

Soulior is currently in an early prototype stage. Screenshots and GIFs may use temporary assets and do not represent the final visual style.
