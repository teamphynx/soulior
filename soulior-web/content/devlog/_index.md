Development notes are published in 2-week sprints. Each entry records what changed, what problems appeared, and what comes next.

Each devlog is meant to document actual work in simple terms:

- Sprint Goal
- What I Built
- What Changed in Soulior
- Technical Notes
- Design Notes
- Problems
- What I Learned
- Prototype Media
- Next Sprint Goal
